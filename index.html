About Robotics

Robotics is the field of engineering that combines mechanical design, electronics, control systems, and programming to build intelligent machines. Robots are used in industries like manufacturing, healthcare, space exploration, defense, and smart automation to improve efficiency and safety.

Sensors

Devices that detect environmental changes like distance, light, temperature, and pressure.

Actuators

Components that enable movement through motors, servos, and hydraulic systems.

Controllers

Microcontrollers and processors that execute commands and manage robot operations.

AI + Automation

Intelligent algorithms that enable decision-making, learning, and autonomous behavior.
